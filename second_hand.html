The best way to get to your destination is by bicycle. Don’t pay to much for a bike, just buy a second hand bike. Here’s a guide so you know what to look for.

1. Stolen

Never buy a stolen product. Because you are committing a crime when you buy a stolen bike. Go to a well known dealer second hand dealership so you know for sure that you’re committing a crime.

2. Purpose

Decide what is going to be the purpose of your bike. Are you only going to use it to get to your destination? Or do you perhaps want use it for sporting purposes? Get your purpose clear before you are going to the bike shop. So you know what sort of bike to look out for.

3. Size

First look at your own size.Then look only to bikes that could fit to your body. After that, you are going to look what is comfortable to you. Most important is to look at the wheels of the bike. Because you can’t adjust them in size like could do with a saddle for instance.

4. Main Parts

Test the main parts like the frame and the handle bar. Do they feel and look solid and good shape? Also check the brakes and lighting. Do the brakes work well? The bike dealer can adjustment the braking sensitivity most of the time.

5. Smooth

Does everything work smooth on your potentially new bike? Do the pedals rotate smooth?Do the wheels spin smooth? To check this, lift one of the wheels and spin it. Look closely to it. Is there any backlash? Don’t buy it.
